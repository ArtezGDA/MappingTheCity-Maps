# Floating screens

## concept

I am interested in the need of screens nowadays. People are really focused on their mobile phones. There is a big fear of having no coverage and being not online. To get all “addicts” satisfied there need to be a lot of cell towers that produce and spread the signal. In my info graphic you can see the amount of cell towers per region.
